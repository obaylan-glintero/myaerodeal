## üé® Screenshot Quality Tips

### Before Taking Screenshots:

1. **Clean Data:**
   - Use realistic company/aircraft names
   - Proper capitalization
   - No "Test" or "Debug" data visible
   - Use actual dollar amounts

2. **Visual Polish:**
   - Choose Light mode (usually photographs better)
   - Full-screen browser (hide bookmarks bar)
   - No notification badges or alerts
   - Hide personal info (email addresses, etc.)

3. **Composition:**
   - Leave some whitespace around edges
   - Capture full cards/sections (don't cut off)
   - Show enough data to be interesting (3-5 items minimum)
   - Keep it organized and clean

### After Taking Screenshots:

1. **Check Quality:**
   - Clear and sharp (not blurry)
   - Text is readable
   - Colors look good
   - No pixelation

2. **Optimize File Size:**
   - Go to https://tinypng.com
   - Upload each screenshot
   - Download compressed version
   - Keep under 200KB per image
